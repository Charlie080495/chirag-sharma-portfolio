Case Studies

Selected work, told in problem → solution → outcome.

A few representative engagements that illustrate how I work — and what changes after I'm done.

Healthcare · Compliance

HIPAA & EVV-Compliant Care Management Platform

Problem

Multiple SaaS healthcare modules needed to demonstrate HIPAA and EVV compliance to enterprise customers and auditors, but documentation was scattered, inconsistent, and difficult to defend during external reviews.

Approach

Led requirements elicitation across product, engineering, and clinical SMEs. Built a unified control framework mapping each compliance requirement to user stories, acceptance criteria, and test cases. Authored audit-ready specs and traceability matrices in Confluence; partnered with BI to wire compliance KPIs into care-manager dashboards.

Outcome

Audit-ready documentation that passed enterprise customer reviews. Care managers and leadership got real-time analytics into compliance posture and care delivery — turning compliance from a cost center into a sales asset.

30% lift in user satisfaction
100+ user stories shipped
HIPAA + EVV aligned
LMS · Roadmap Ownership

Learn2Care LMS — Roadmap & Strategy

Problem

Learn2Care needed a strategic roadmap that aligned engineering capacity with market opportunity — without losing the day-to-day stakeholder requests competing for the same backlog.

Approach

Ran a structured roadmap discovery: competitor teardown, market research interviews, and stakeholder workshops with Product, Sales, and Customer Success. Translated themes into a Now / Next / Later roadmap with clear success metrics and prioritized backlog tied to each theme.

Outcome

A roadmap leadership could stand behind in customer conversations and that engineering could plan releases around — replacing reactive ticket triage with intentional sequencing tied to business strategy.

Owned end-to-end roadmap
Multi-stakeholder alignment
Strategy-led backlog
AI-Augmented · Process

AI-Augmented BA Workflow with Claude

Problem

Documentation cycles were eating into discovery and validation time. Stakeholder interviews, BRD drafts, and edge-case enumeration took days that could have gone to higher-leverage work.

Approach

Designed a repeatable BA workflow using Claude: structured prompts for first-draft user stories and BRDs, edge-case stress-testing checklists, test scenario generation, and stakeholder interview summarization. Built a manual validation step at every handoff so AI accelerates without replacing judgement.

Outcome

Documentation turnaround dropped roughly 50%. Reclaimed time went into deeper requirement validation, sharper UAT plans, and earlier risk discovery — quality stayed high because every output was reviewed.

~50% faster documentation
Validated, never blind
Repeatable playbook
SaaS · API Strategy

AI-Powered Assessment SaaS — API & Data Schema

Problem

A SaaS assessment product was bottlenecked by ad-hoc third-party integrations. Each new partner integration was bespoke, slow, and brittle — limiting scalability.

Approach

Worked with engineering to define standardized API contracts and data schemas. Mapped every external integration to a shared surface area, documented authentication, error handling, and versioning expectations. Wrote integration-ready specs that partners could self-onboard against.

Outcome

Partner integrations moved from custom one-offs to a repeatable contract. Engineering reclaimed sprint capacity, and the product gained a scalable integration story for sales conversations.

Scalable integration surface
Self-serve partner specs
40% manual effort reduced
